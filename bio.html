Early Life

Hazrat Allama Ilyas Attar Qadri was born on 26th Ramadan, 1369 Hijri (1950) in Karachi, Pakistan. He belonged to a Memon family known for honesty and business ethics. From a young age, he showed interest in Islamic knowledge and spirituality.

Formation of Dawat-e-Islami

In 1981, with the support of Islamic scholars and spiritual mentors, he founded Dawat-e-Islami. Starting from a small Masjid, this movement now spreads across over 195 countries, with thousands of madaris and Jamia-tul-Madina campuses.

Simplicity and Love for Sunnah

Despite his global influence, Ilyas Qadri is known for his simplicity, humility, and love for every Muslim. He emphasizes the importance of beard, turban, miswak, and punctuality in Salah.
