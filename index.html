Sheikh-e-Tariqat, Ameer-e-Ahl-e-Sunnat Hazrat Allama Maulana Muhammad Ilyas Attar Qadri is the founder of Dawat-e-Islami – a global Islamic movement. His life is dedicated to spreading the teachings of the Qur’an, Sunnah, and love for the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.
This website explores his biography, teachings, and efforts to revive Islamic values worldwide.
